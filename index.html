Why Us.

Internatinal students often feel overwhelmed cause of the lack of knowledge about the culture of the country they are in. Etiquette Trainer lets you learn the slangs, culture for the country you are moving to by letting you go through levels.

Our aim

The aim of Etiquette Trainer is let every International student have a comfortable life in the country they are living in by knowing their social lifestyle, slangs, norms and etiquettes.

Advantages

As an Internatinal student we go thorugh many problems, and one of the problems is to live like locals, able to understand their way of living to know their slangs and norms. So we have created an app for you which lets you solve this lack of knowledge. The advantages of this app are manifold, some of them are:

Social life improves.

When you know the norms then you have more social interaction and your social life eventually improves.

Students gets a confidence boost.

As an Internatinal student, everything is new to us and some part of us is always hesitant. When you know norms and etiquettes then you kind of have an idea and feel somewhat confident.

Chances of getting accepted improves

When you know things, then locals also feels like you are one of them and your chances of getting accpeted improves.

Overall get a smooth experience in the country.

When you have an idea of how to live in a new country, then you will have an awesome experience in the country you are moving to.
